📚 Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

JEE Main / GUJCET Examination

Appear for JEE Main (for Engineering) or GUJCET (Gujarat Common Entrance Test). Your percentile/rank will be the basis for admission.

2

Registration on ACPC Portal

Register on the official ACPC website with valid credentials. Fill in personal, academic, and contact details accurately.

3

Document Verification

Upload and verify all required documents including mark sheets, caste certificate (if applicable), domicile certificate, etc.

4

Choice Filling

Fill your college and branch preferences carefully. You can fill up to 1000+ choices based on your rank and category.

5

Seat Allotment

Based on your rank, category, and choices, seats are allotted in multiple rounds. Check results on the ACPC portal.

6

Fee Payment & Reporting

Pay the admission fee online and report to the allotted college within the specified deadline with original documents.

📃 Required Documents

✅ Mandatory Documents

  • ✅ JEE Main / GUJCET Scorecard
  • ✅ 10th Standard Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • ✅ 12th Standard Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • ✅ School Leaving Certificate (LC)
  • ✅ Domicile Certificate (for Home State quota)
  • ✅ Aadhar Card
  • ✅ Passport Size Photographs
  • ✅ Character Certificate

❗Category-Based Documents

  • ❗Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
  • ❗Caste Validity Certificate
  • ❗Non-Creamy Layer Certificate (OBC)
  • ❗Income Certificate (EWS)
  • ❗Disability Certificate (PwD)
  • ❗Sports Certificate (Sports quota)
  • ❗Defense Personnel Certificate (Defense quota)
  • ❗Migration Certificate (for other state students)

Important Tips & Guidelines

Choice Filling Strategy

Fill maximum choices (1000+) in order of your true preference. Include safe, moderate, and dream colleges to maximize your chances.

Meet Deadlines

Never miss deadlines for registration, choice filling, or fee payment. Late submissions are not entertained under any circumstances.

Document Verification

Keep all documents ready in both original and photocopy format. Ensure certificates are attested by appropriate authorities.

Seat Acceptance

If you get a seat, you must accept it online and pay fees to freeze the seat. You can still participate in next rounds for upgrades.
